From SWOT to Persona-Ready Ideas

Assessment 1 Preparation Activity
This worksheet helps you link your macro scan (PESTLE, SWOT) to segmentation, Jobs to Be Done, and a robust persona. Use evidence for every point—data and references are required for assessment.

Why we are doing this

This activity prepares you for Assessment 1 Sections 3–4. Demonstrate your ability to build a logical chain from environmental scan through to persona, using LaundryMax Co. as the practice brand.

What you will demonstrate

  • Build a referenced SWOT (separate S/W/O/T, 3 points each, with sources)
  • Apply segmentation: four-lens model (with evidence)
  • Develop three-layer Jobs to Be Done (importance, satisfaction, evidence)
  • Construct a high-impact persona for innovation concept

Evidence at Every Step

  • Support each point and segment with a referenced source
  • Include at least one measurable outcome in persona
  • Export your completed worksheet to Word (see instructions at end)

How to Complete

  1. Work through the sections below
  2. Fill in each row—be specific, reference evidence
  3. Export to Word and include your completed worksheet in your assessment
