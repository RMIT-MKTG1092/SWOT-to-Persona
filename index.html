From SWOT to Persona-Ready Ideas

Assessment 1 Preparation Activity
This page helps you link your macro scan (PESTLE, SWOT) to segmentation, Jobs to Be Done, and a robust persona. Use evidence for every point—data and references are required for assessment.

Why we are doing this

This activity prepares you to complete Assessment 1 Sections 3–4. You’ll demonstrate your ability to build a logical chain from environmental scan through to persona, using LaundryMax Co. as the practice brand.

What you will demonstrate

  • Build a referenced SWOT (2x2 matrix with sources)
  • Apply segmentation: four-lens model (with evidence)
  • Develop three-layer Jobs to Be Done (plus importance, satisfaction, evidence)
  • Construct a high-impact persona for innovation concept

Required: Evidence for Every Step

  • Support each SWOT, segment, and job with a referenced source (report, article, dataset, lecture, etc)
  • Include at least one measurable outcome in persona
  • Export your completed worksheet to Word (see template below)

How to Complete

  1. Work through the sections below (one card at a time)
  2. Fill in each row/box—be specific, reference evidence
  3. Click Export to Word and include your completed matrix in Assessment 1
